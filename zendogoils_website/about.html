Maria de Schneider

Dog lover and herbal remedy enthusiast has fused her two passions in order to formulate this aromatic blend of animal friendly essential oils.

As Edgewater New Jersey’s prominent dog walker, Maria has spent the last 2 years tending to her diverse client base of furry friends. According to her pedometer, Maria averages 6 miles of walking each day as she traverses this NJ town along the Hudson River.

As a mother of two daughters, she has perfected the art of patience and compassion and demonstrates both in her care for her four legged children including Bella, Max, Luna, Lola, Pecabo, Jeter, Jordan, Rocky, and etc. Maria has been inspired to help improve the quality of life and health of man’s best friend.

Prior to working in pet care services, Maria spent 30+ years working in the fashion industry as a make-up artist. As a make-up artist, Maria had the opportunity to travel the world and work with an extensive list of celebrities, models, and artists. Maria was born in Caracas, Venezuela and can speak English, Spanish, and Italian.
